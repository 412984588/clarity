## Overview

Clarity is an AI-powered problem-solving assistant. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the app.

---

## Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Email address, authentication provider (email, Google, Apple).
- **User Content**: Text you submit during problem-solving sessions.
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, feature usage, and session metadata.
- **Device & Diagnostics**: Device type, OS version, crash logs (if enabled).
- **Support Communications**: Emails or messages you send to support.
- **Payments**: Billing and subscription data handled by third-party payment processors.

---

## How We Use Information

- Provide and operate the service.
- Improve product quality and reliability.
- Provide customer support.
- Prevent abuse and maintain security.
- Process subscriptions and payments.

## Sharing and Third Parties

We may share data with service providers that help us run the app, such as:

- Payment processors
- Analytics or monitoring providers (if enabled)
