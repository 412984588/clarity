## What is Solacore?

Solacore is a mobile app that acts as your thinking partner when you're stuck on a decision or facing a challenge. It guides you through a structured 5-step framework:

1. **Receive**: Describe your problem or challenge
2. **Clarify**: Unpack the situation with AI-powered questions
3. **Reframe**: See your challenge from new perspectives
4. **Options**: Explore solutions with AI suggestions
5. **Commit**: Choose a next action and move forward

The app integrates with OpenAI and Claude to provide intelligent, context-aware guidance while keeping conversations private and secure.

## Do & Don't (Sending Best Practices)

### ‚úÖ DO

- **Personalize each message**: Add a line about why you chose this person ("I know you're good at X, so I'd love your perspective")
- **Test the APK link first**: Download and install yourself before sending to others
- **Update the build date**: If you send a new build, mention "New build from Dec 24 with bug fixes"
- **Set expectations clearly**: "This is rough beta software - expect bugs"
- **Make it easy to say no**: "If you're too busy, no problem at all!"
- **Follow up politely**: 1 reminder after 3 days, 1 after 7 days, then stop
- **Thank every tester**: Even if they only tested for 5 minutes
- **Share updates**: When you fix their reported bugs, let them know!

### ‚ùå DON'T

- **Spam the same message to 50 people**: Personalize at least the first line
- **Send broken links**: Always test before sending
- **Overpromise**: Don't say "This will change your life" - set realistic expectations
- **Guilt trip**: Don't say "I spent months on this, please test it"
- **Ask for feedback too soon**: Give testers 3-5 days to try the app first
- **Send without context**: Don't just send an APK link with no explanation
- **Ignore feedback**: If someone reports a bug, acknowledge it within 24 hours
- **Forget to remove inactive testers**: After 2 reminders, stop bothering them
